## ðŸ”§ Technical Solution: Fase Scheiding

### **Fase 1: Canvas Foundation Workflows**

#### **1. Problem Exploration Workflow**
**Gebaseerd op Toolkit Worksheets:**
- **Issue Definition:** What-Why-Where-Who-When-How-How Often analyse
- **Selecting Your Problem:** Conditions assessment en problem choice rationale

**Output:** Duidelijk gedefinieerd probleem met stakeholder impact assessment

#### **2. Envisioned Future Development**
**Toolkit Integration:**
- **Future Vision Crafting:** Ideal situation articulation
- **Success Metrics Definition:** Concrete outcome specifications

**Output:** Inspirerende toekomstvisie die richting geeft aan alle andere elementen

#### **3. Strategic Purpose Crafting**
**Toolkit Basis:**
- **Pains & Gains:** Stakeholder impact analysis
- **Dimensions of Value:** Multi-perspective value assessment (freedom from need, just relations, human dignity, etc.)
- **Value Trade-offs:** Balanced purpose formulation

**Output:** Meetbare purpose statement met waarde alignment

#### **4. Team & Environment Assessment**
**Gecombineerde Elementen:**
- **Extended Team & Logics of Engagement:** Stakeholder identification met engagement drivers
- **Understanding Issue History:** Environmental context analysis (political, economic, social trends)
- **Orchestrating Team:** Core team composition en governance

**Output:** Stakeholder ecosystem met governance structuur

#### **5. Strategy Development**
**Canvas Element Integration:**
- **What Ifs Sketch:** Alternative solution exploration
- **Capacity Assessment:** Resources & expertise inventory
- **Support Assessment:** Political authorization en public spirit mapping
- **Public Value Proposition:** Core hypothesis formulation

**Output:** Uitvoeringsstrategie met resource alignment

#### **6. Value Specification**
**Advanced Canvas Elements:**
- **Public Value Account:** Benefits vs costs T-account ledger
- **Value Proposition:** Concrete service offering definition
- **Canvas Checklist:** Completeness validation (Credible, Coherent, Complete)

**Output:** Gebalanceerde business case met stakeholder commitment

### **Fase 2: Ecosystem Realization Workflows**

#### **Ecosystem Design Workflow (Post-Canvas)**
**Wetenschappelijke Uitwerking:**
- **Performance Monitoring System:** Evidence-based KPIs (SNA, collaboration metrics, ecosystem health)
- **Risk Assessment & Mitigation:** Comprehensive risk frameworks
- **Multi-level Governance:** Institutional design gebaseerd op wetenschappelijke literatuur
- **Process Integration:** Cross-actor workflow design
- **Technology Architecture:** Integration principles en federated systems

**Input:** Gevalideerd PVC + design readiness assessment
**Output:** Uitvoerbaar ecosystem implementatie plan
